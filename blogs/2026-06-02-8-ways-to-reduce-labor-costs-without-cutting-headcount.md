Replacing a single frontline worker costs between $6,500 and $7,000, representing roughly 40% of annual compensation, and organizations with 10,000 frontline employees at 45% annual turnover face tens of millions in yearly expenses. This guide outlines eight strategies targeting turnover, vacancy periods, administrative recruiting tasks, and compliance penalties — none involving wage reductions or staffing cuts.
