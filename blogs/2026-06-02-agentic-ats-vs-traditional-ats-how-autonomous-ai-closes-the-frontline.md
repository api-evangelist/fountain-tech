Traditional ATS platforms were designed for lower hiring volumes and desktop-based applications, while agentic ATS systems actively execute screening, interviews, onboarding, and re-engagement without human intervention at each step. According to Fountain's 2025 Frontline Report, 74% of frontline workers prefer voice interviews and 60% abandon applications that are too long or poorly optimized for mobile.
